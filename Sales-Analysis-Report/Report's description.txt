This interactive report is designed to provide you with comprehensive insights into sales performance across various dimensions. The data can be segmented by regions, visualized and compared performance from West, East, Central and South. We can navigate through the sales data over specific time periods and also examine sales data by product subcategories. Quickly access information about overall sales figures: total sales, profit, quantity and discount.

This dashboard is an essential tool for sales managers, marketing teams, and business analysts seeking to drive sales growth and improve overall performance.
